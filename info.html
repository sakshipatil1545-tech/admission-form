Admission Guidelines

Welcome to the admission process. Please read the guidelines carefully before applying:

  • Fill out the admission form completely.
  • Attach all necessary documents (marksheets, ID proof, etc.).
  • Submit the form before the deadline.
  • Wait for the confirmation email.

Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Passed Junior college with a minimum of 45%
  • Valid ID proof required
